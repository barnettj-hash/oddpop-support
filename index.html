Troubleshooting

Fast fixes

The game opens to the wrong screen

Fully close and relaunch OddPop. The normal app launch should open to the Game Hub first, where every game can be selected.

I cannot find Prism Paths

Prism Paths is in the Game Hub. Scroll down if it is not visible right away.

Music or sound is quiet

Check media volume, silent mode, and the in-app settings for music and sound effect volume.

My best score changed

Scores and settings are stored on the device. Reinstalling the app or clearing device storage can reset local progress.

Game Center is not connected

Open Settings in OddPop, connect Game Center, then return to the Game Hub and play again.
