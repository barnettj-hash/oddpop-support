Privacy Policy

Local play, clear controls.

OddPop Arcade is built for quick play. The app uses local storage for scores, settings, themes, and progress.

Back to Support Privacy Question

1. Information OddPop stores

OddPop stores gameplay progress on the device, including best scores, daily challenge scores, settings, unlocked themes, and unlocked achievements.

2. Accounts

OddPop does not require an app account to play. If Game Center is used, Apple handles that sign-in and Game Center experience.

3. Game Center

When enabled, OddPop may submit supported scores and achievements to Game Center. Game Center is optional and can be managed through Apple account settings.

4. Permissions

OddPop uses local audio and haptics for gameplay feedback. The current app does not require location, contacts, camera, or microphone access.

5. Analytics and advertising

The current version does not use third-party analytics SDKs or third-party advertising SDKs. OddPop does not sell personal data.

6. Support requests

Support requests can be opened on the support page: barnettj-hash.github.io/oddpop-support/support.html . Public GitHub issues should not include sensitive personal information.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated if OddPop adds cloud sync, analytics, or other data-related features.
